To get to Papingo from Aristi you drive the winding road to the bottom of the Vikos Gorge, cross the Voidomatis river and drive up, up, up the switchbacks. The road is stunning and beautiful with views that keep improving with every turn.

  • Aristi nature lives in harmony with human craftsmanship. Aristi's stone walls and hand hewn wooden beams are perched high above the Vikos Gorge.
